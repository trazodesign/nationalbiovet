Anaerobic Culture

Test Code: 404
Specimen: Specimen should be in anaerobic transport medium (a thin smear for gram stain obtained from the same site as the culture is strongly recommended)

Acid Fast Stain

Test Code: 415
Specimen: Specimen in sterile plastic container

Specimen: A culture or collection swab or in a sterile container

AFB Identification Only

Specimen: A culture or collection swab or in a sterile container

Specimen: A culture or collection swab or in a sterile container

AFB Sensitivity Only

Specimen: A culture or collection swab or in a sterile container

AFB Smear

Blood Culture

Test Code: 402
Specimen: 2 Blood Culture bottles each culture collectio

Chlamydia Culture

Test Code: N5029
Specimen: Use Dacron or Rayon swabs (Do not use wood shafted swabs, calcium alginate or cotton swabs) or peasized fresh feces placed directly into the Chlamydia transport medium and emulsified.

Culture & Sensitivities

Test Code: 401
Specimen: A culture collection swab or in a sterile screw-capped container.

Fungal Culture Skin & Nails

Test Code: 403
Specimen: Nail Scraping should be from a subsurface portion of the infected nail. Skin should be taken from the active border of lesion. Transport specimen in sterile container.

Gram Stain

Test Code: 408
Specimen: Carefully select material from infected area with a sterile swab. Gently roll swab onto clean slide to make a thin smear. Air dry do not fix.

Ova - Parasites

Test Code: 506
Specimen: Feces

Stool Culture Screen

Test Code: 405
Specimen: Submit feces in a clean container. May use rectal swabs or a swab of fecal material collected with culture Collection swab.

Stool Culture Comprehensive Isolation For Salmonella, Shigella, Campylobacter & Yersinia

Test Code: 406
Specimen: Specimen is the same as the stool culture screen.

Stool Occult Blood

Test Code: 503
Specimen: Random fecal specimen
